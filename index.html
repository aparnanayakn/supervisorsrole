Navigating Your Supervision Journey: A Guide for Success

Dissertations/projects typically carry a significant credit allocation and have a workload associated with them which can span more than one semester and/or the complete academic session. Dissertation and projects are usually a significant demonstration of programme learning outcomes. Supervisors should provide regular, structured guidance and feedback to support students in planning, researching, and writing their dissertation/project, ensuring academic standards are maintained and learning outcomes are met. Students should be provided with written guidelines to outline and assist in completion of their dissertation/project.
